Frequently Asked Questions

How does the monthly usage reset work?

Your monthly usage limits reset on the same date each month that you started your subscription. For example, if you subscribed on the 15th, your limits reset on the 15th of every month.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new limits take effect immediately. When downgrading, changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What happens to unused credits?

Credits don't roll over to the next month. Each feature has its own monthly limit that resets according to your billing cycle.

Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?

Absolutely! Our Professional plan can be customized for large teams and enterprise needs. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.
